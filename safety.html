Safety isn't a feature here.

It's the reason parents choose us — and residents stay. Here's exactly what we do at every property.

Our first design principle

What "safe" actually means at Aadhya

  • 24×7 CCTV at entrances and common areas, at every property
  • Controlled entry — visitors don't walk in unannounced
  • On-site caretaker at each building, with escalation to central management
  • Established residential streets — our buildings are in lived-in, well-lit neighbourhoods, not isolated plots
  • Separate, secure floors at our co-living property
  • Responsive management — one WhatsApp message reaches people who can act

Parents: visit us first.

Before your daughter or son moves in, come see the building, meet the caretaker, taste the food. We'll arrange a visit the same day in most cases.
